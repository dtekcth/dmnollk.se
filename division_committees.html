Studying at Chalmers

Presidiet (part of the division board)

Presidiet consists of 5 different posts, included in the division board. The president, vice president, treasurer, secretary and SAMO (student welfare manager of the division) of the division are the positions in this committee, which does board-things.
Email address: presidiet@dtek.se

DNS

Is there a problem with a course? In that case DNS is the committee to talk to! DNS attends course meetings for both the bachelor program as well as the master programs for Computer Science and tries to improve the courses you are taking.
Email address: dns@dtek.se

DAG

All contact between the division and companies interested in recruiting Computer Science students goes through DAG. DAG mainly arranges pub evenings and lunch lectures togther with companies.
Email address: dag@dtek.se

D6

D6 arranges larger 'sittningar' (translates to (usually) three course dinners) as well as 'gasques' from time to time during the year. 'Gasquen' is Chalmers' night club and committees similar to D6 of Chalmers divisions takes turn in arranging it.
Email address: d6@dtek.se

DRust

DRust takes care of Basen and makes sure everything in it is working and is clean. Basen is the CSE division's own locale where you can study, cook, heat your lunch or occasionally attend pubs. You can also borrow tools from DRust if you need it at home.
Email address: drust@dtek.se

Delta

The division's committee for arranging pubs is Delta. Delta arranges pubs for the division and during the pub crawl, which happens the first Thursday in every study period.
Email address: delta@dtek.se

DNollK

DNollK is the reception committee for the students starting Computer Science' bachelor program and is arranging lots of different events.
Email address: dnollk@dtek.se

DKock

Hungry? Then make sure to attend DKock's events! DKock is the division's own cooking committee and arranges barbeque evenings and helps other committees to cook for their events.
Email address: dkock@dtek.se

iDrott

iDrott arranges sport events and usually plays games and sports, as well as goes out running once a week. It is also possible to borrow sport things such as balls and 'kubb' from them.
Email address: idrott@dtek.se

DBus

The division has its own minivan and you can rent it for a very small amount of money! The bus is taken care of by DBus.
Email address: dbus@dtek.se

Talhenspresidiet

Talhenspresidiet organises the meetings of the division where decisions affecting the entire division and all its students are taken. Everyone at the divsion are welcome to attend the meetings! A representative from the Student Union also attends these meetings and presents information from Chalmers.
Email address: talhenspresidiet@dtek.se
