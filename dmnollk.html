DMNollK 2019

Chairman

Angelica "Angie" Strandberg

Angelica is the chairman of the committé and thereby the one to make sure everyone in the committé is doing what they should. She also attends meetings with other chairmen of the committés at the division and sends meeting invitations.

PR manager

Johan "JoJo" Johansson

Whenever there is a PR in the form of a poster or Facebook-event or something else to be published informing of the different events we arrange, JoJo is the one to create and publish them. He also manages the Instagram- and LinkedIn-account.

Module manager

Shahnur Isgandarli

The module you hopefully have read already (otherwise make sure to do so! It can be found under the "Module"-tab above) was written by Shahnur. The module describes all fun events during the master reception as well as giving some information about Chalmers and the Computer Science and Engineering division.

Phadder manager

Manoj Reddy

Manoj is responsible for recruting phadders. The phadders' roll is to help us out arranging the master reception, whilst at the same time enjoying all the events together with the new students at a masterprogram at the CSE division.

Student Welfare manager

Algot Nilsson

If you need to contact someone and ask questions about the division, Chalmers or perhaps wants to know where you can find more information about moving to Sweden, Algot is the one to talk to. He can also help you contact the student welfare manager of the division (SAMO), who in turn can help you contact for example the university cleric (who is also the welfare officer) or the healthcare for students (Akademihälsan).

Event Manager

Deepak Desai

Deepak's roll as the event manager is to make sure the logistics around all events is working. He also makes sure all events are wellplanned before-hand and that everyone in the committé knows what to do and when during the events.

Food manager

Philip "Phax" Svensson

The food manager of DMNollK is Phax. Apart from writing shopping lists and planning all the delicious food we cook, he is also responsible for contacting other committés for borrowing things or even hire them to make sure the food supertasty.
