Interested in joining a division committée?

Awesome! Every year the committées changes who is in the committée and before that (almost) all committées have a so called "aspning". This is an opportunity to find out more about the committées and what they are doing and at the same time getting to know other students who are also interested in joining. It is also totally fine to attend the "aspning" also without applying for the committée. Most committées starts their "aspning" in January, but a few has it already during the autumn, depending on when applications for the respective committée is to be made. Contact the committée you are interested in if you want to know when they start recruiting! Applications is done at division meetings.

Interested in joining a committée at the Student Union?

That's great! To find out more about the committé and when its "aspning" is, contact the committée you are interested in. "Aspning" is an opportunity to find out more about the committées and what they are doing and at the same time getting to know other students who are also interested in joining. It is also totally fine to attend the "aspning" also without applying for the committée.

Are you missing a committée?

Doesn't there seem to be a committée at the division or Student Union for what you are interested in? Then start the committée yourself! If you want to start a committée at the division, you need to send in a paper describing the committée and its purpose to the committée Talhenspresidiet and to the divsion board (see emails in the committée list). If you want to start it at the Student Union contact the Student Union management team to find out how.
