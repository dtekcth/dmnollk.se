Committees at Chalmers

There are lots of committees at Chalmers in the Student Union as well as at the CSE division! There are committees for many, many different interests, like for example dancing, singing, playing football, arranging parties or pubs or taking care of the division's rooms. If you can't find a committee for what you are interested in, you can also create your own! Check out the current committees at the division in the different submenus for committees above.

Interested in joining a division committee?

Awesome! Every year the committees changes who is in the committee and before that (almost) all committees have a so called 'aspning'. This is an opportunity to find out more about the committees and what they are doing and at the same time getting to know other students who are also interested in joining. It is also totally fine to attend the 'aspning' also without applying for the committee. Most committees starts their 'aspning' in January, but a few has it already during the autumn, depending on when applications for the respective committee is to be made. Contact the committee you are interested in if you want to know when they start recruiting! Applications is generally done at division meetings.

Interested in joining a committee at the Student Union?

That's great! To find out more about the committee and when its 'aspning' is, contact the committee you are interested in. 'Aspning' is an opportunity to find out more about the committees and what they are doing and at the same time getting to know other students who are also interested in joining. It is also totally fine to attend the 'aspning' also without applying for the committee. A list of committees at the Student Union can be found here .

Are you missing a committee?

Doesn't there seem to be a committee at the division or Student Union for what you are interested in? Then start the committee yourself! If you want to start a committee at the division, you need to send in a paper describing the committee and its purpose to the committee Talhenspresidiet and to the divsion board (see emails in the committee list). If you want to start it at the Student Union contact the Student Union management team to find out how.
