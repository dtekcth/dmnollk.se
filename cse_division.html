Dtek - the division webpage

Did you know that the division has it's own webpage ? On the webpage you will find all sorts of important and useful information, such as an event calendar saying for example when the next D6 sittning will be or when iDrott will play football and badminton. From the webpage you can also subscribe to the weekly newsletter to get updates about the different events happening at the division during the week.

Photos from events

During most of the events at the division, DFoto is there to take photos of the event and its guests. The pictures can be found at the DFoto-webpage .
