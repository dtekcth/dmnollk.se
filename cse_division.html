Studying at Chalmers

Buying second hand books

The CSE division has its own Facebook group for selling and buying course literature one doesn't need anymore. You can find lots of books there! The group is called Bokbytargrupp för data (despite that the name is in Swedish, you may of course write in English in the group).

Dtek - the division webpage

Did you know that the division has it's own webpage? On the CSE-webpage ? you will find all sorts of important and useful information, such as an event calendar saying for example when the next D6 sittning will be or when iDrott will play football and badminton. From the webpage you can also subscribe to the weekly newsletter to get updates about the different events happening at the division during the week.

Basen and how to book it

Basen is the division room for Computer Science and in there you can cook and microwave your lunch as well as study. From time to time, there are also different events in there such as pubs, sittnings and game nights. If you would like to arrange something in Basen, you may book it. In order to do so, just fill in the form .

Renting the division car

Did you know that the division has its own car and that it's available for anyone at Chalmers to rent? Bonus: the cost for renting is really low, especially for members of the division, i.e. if you study the bachelor program for Computer Science, MPALG, MPCSN or MPHPC! You find more information about renting it here .

Division meetings

All students at the CSE division are welcome to attend the division meetings where lots of important decisions affecting all the divsion's students are taken. Information from the Student Union is also given. The meetings are held by the committee Talhenspresidiet. Free food and 'fika' are available during the meeting and usually there is a lottery as well where someone at the meeting wins a cool thing! The meetings are held at the Thursday in study week 6 every study period plus a few extra times a year.
Follow the CSE division's Facebook page and check the notice boards in Basen to see when the next meeting is!

Photos from events

During most of the events at the division, DFoto is there to take photos of the event and its guests. The pictures can be found at the DFoto-webpage .

The Dtek wikipage

There is also a wikipage for Computer Science. Among other things, you can read the protocols from the division meetings there. You can have a look at it here . Note that an account is needed to be able to view most of the information.

Do something fun for the division!

Do you have a great idea of something fun to do for the students at the division? Perhaps you would like to bake a cake or buy something to be used by all students at the CSE division? In that case, there is a possibility to ask for money for it from the division board! All you have to do is filling in the form for it and cross your fingers, hoping they will say yes!
