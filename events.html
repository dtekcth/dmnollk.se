The following events are only for the first year master students at MPALG, MPCSN and MPHPC.

WELCOME EVENT


August 29th
Free event
No signup
17:17 at Deltaparken

During your first day you will get a lot of information about your Master's program and the courses you will have. Take the chance to restore your brain and just relax and hang out with some of your new classmates at the Delta Park after your first classes. The park is located just outside of Basen, the place where you as a student of the computer division, will have access to and is always welcome to study or use our microwaves and kitchen. While you bask in the dubious sun getting to know one another, DMNollK will be handing out some juicy food (vegan choice available) and beverages. Moreover, we will have a quiz walk / treasure hunt to show you the campus and introduce you to some of the other committees at our department.

CLIMBING


September 2nd
Free to attend
Limited spots, make sure to sign up!
19:45-22:00 at Fysiken Klätterlabbet Centrum

Bad weather? Yes, no? Who cares when you can take outside inside! Look for the big oxidized copper dome on the edge of campus and join us for some climbing. Beginner or pro, come as you are! Prepare to get a bit sweaty, so grab your workout clothes if you have any. They have a limited number of climbing shoes available to borrow, so bring climbing shoes if you have any, otherwise any normal indoor-gym shoes would also work. There are changing rooms and showers for those who wishes. Come join us in an evening of walls, sweat and tears.

INTERNATIONAL SITTNING


September 3rd
Small cost to attend, payment at the entrance
Limited tickets, make sure to sign up!
18:00 at Basen

Sittings are one of the oldest traditions at Chalmers and this one comes with a delicious three course dinner! The wonderful cooking committee DKock will be making the food and when that happens, nothing can go wrong! You will learn the rules of a sitting and maybe do something that may resemble singing. All is prepared for your enjoyment and the toastmasters will make sure that the evening is unforgettable!

TRIP TO DELSJÖN


September 5th
Free event
No signup
Meet 17:17 at Basen, then we will take the tram together

Gothenburg is actually surrounded by an amazing environment. It is not only close to the sea but also near lush forests and clear lakes. We will meet near Basen where we will take the public transportation to the area of Delsjön. Feel free to bring your guitar or favorite outdoors game! We will also organize some food for you. Stay tuned and remember to bring clothes appropriate for the weather!

GAME NIGHT


September 7th
Free event
No signup, first come first served
17:17 at Basen

The committee called DLude will supply us with some awesome games. There will also be hot dogs and cold beverages. You're more than welcome to stop by our game night to play games that you already love, or maybe to try some new games and make frenemies forever.

CRAYFISH PARTY


September 9th
50 SEK, payment at the entrance
Limited tickets, make sure to sign up!
18:00 at Tågvagnen

A crayfish party is a classic Swedish tradition, taking place during late summer. You don't want to start your second week as a master's student without singing hilarious songs while trying to figure out how to eat the delicious, red, crazy looking shellfish! Crayfish is the main star of this evening but you will also be served side dishes along with a nice sip of traditional Swedish snaps that goes down well with the the food! Of course there will be a vegan alternative too, so make sure to be there either way! This is the sittning with the big S. Get a ticket as soon as possible as there are only a limited amount!
