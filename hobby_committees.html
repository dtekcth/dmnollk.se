Studying at Chalmers

DMNollK

DMNollK arranges the master reception and is responsible for this webpage. More information about us can be found under the DMNollK tab above.
Email address: dmnollk@dtek.se

DFoto

DFoto takes photos during the division's events, all of which you can find at dfoto.se .
Email address: dfoto@dtek.se

dHack

Every week, dHack invites all students at the division to help out with the students own programming projects or possibly school work and gives away 'fika'. dHack is also responsible for all IT-systems at the division.
Email address: dhack@dtek.se

Dlude

Dlude organises game nights where you can come and play a whole bunch of different board games and buy food cheaply.
Email address: dlude@dtek.se

JämställD

JämställD works for equality amongst everyone at the division and that everyone should feel welcome to the division and feel good.
Email address: jamstallD@dtek.se

D-lirium

D-lirium writes the division magazine, which is printed a few times a year.
Email address: d-lirium@dtek.se

DBrus

DBrus makes podcasts about pretty much anything. Contact them if you want them to talk about a subject you are interested in! The podcasts can be found here: dbrus.dtek.se
Note: all podcasts are in Swedish.
Email address: dbrus@dtek.se

Ståthållarämbetet

During the meetings of the division and parades at Chalmers (such as 'Cortégen'), Ståthållarämbetet participates and carries the division flag.
Email address: stathallarambetet@dtek.se
